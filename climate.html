Climate Action - SDG 13

Urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term changes in temperature and weather patterns, mainly due to human activities.

Why is Climate Action important?

It’s vital to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, prevent natural disasters, and protect vulnerable ecosystems.

How does Climate Action contribute to SDG?

Climate action helps achieve other SDGs by promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impacts.

Did you know?

Global temperatures have risen by approximately 1.2°C since the late 19th century.

Fact

2030 is the year by which we must halve our carbon emissions to avoid catastrophic warming.
